How to buy $ADA

  • Purchase SOL from a reputable exchange:

    Begin by signing up for an account on a trusted cryptocurrency exchange such as Coinbase, Gemini, or Binance. Once your account is verified, deposit fiat currency (like USD) into your exchange account. Navigate to the trading section and buy Solana (SOL) using the deposited funds. Make sure to follow the exchange's guidelines for purchasing SOL, including verification requirements and trading fees.

  • Get the Phantom wallet

    Obtain the Phantom wallet by visiting https://phantom.app or downloading the Phantom wallet app from your device's app store. Follow the prompts to install and set up your wallet. During the setup process, create a strong passphrase and securely store your recovery phrase. Upon completion, you'll receive a unique wallet address associated with your Phantom wallet.

  • Transfer SOL to your Phantom wallet

    From your exchange account, initiate a withdrawal of SOL to your Phantom wallet address. Confirm the accuracy of the wallet address before proceeding with the withdrawal. After the SOL is successfully transferred to your Phantom wallet, access decentralized exchanges compatible with Solana, such as Jupiter or Raydium, through your Phantom wallet interface. Connect your Phantom wallet to the chosen decentralized exchange and navigate to the trading section. Select the SOL/$ADA trading pair, specify the desired amount of SOL to swap for $ADA tokens, review the transaction details, including gas fees, and confirm the swap. Upon confirmation, the $ADA tokens will be credited to your Phantom wallet.

Frequently Asked Questions

"ADA on Sol" (often styled as ADA or ADASOL) is a Solana-based memecoin created as a tribute to Ada, the late dog of Solana co-founder Anatoly Yakovenko. Anatoly frequently shared about his dog Ada on social media, making her a beloved figure in the Solana community (RIP Ada). The token honors her memory and her informal role in the "Solana Pack," with proceeds or themes sometimes tied to supporting dog shelters.

$ADA is a community tribute to honor Ada's memory, the shelter dog Toly rescued and loved. He holds a portion of the supply through community sends, and our charity work aligns with his wish to help shelter animals. No official endorsement exists, but the story is deeply inspired by his original post.

Join the movement by walking or volunteering at local shelters, donating supplies, or sharing adoption stories online. Follow Ada's Second Chance Squad updates, participate in community spaces, and help spread awareness. Every action supports real impact for shelter dogs in Ada's name.
