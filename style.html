Imagery Guidelines (Real Shelter Dogs & Authentic Only)

Exclusively use real shelter dog photos from the adaonsol.com Gallery, official logos, or professionally commissioned artist illustrations.
No AI-generated content, no stock photos, no generic vectors.
These gallery images showcase real rescue dogs – use them for emotional impact, adoption spotlights, and charity-focused content.

Treat these images with care: natural lighting, emotional expressions, no heavy filters. Crop to focus on the dog’s face/eyes for maximum heart connection. Pair with captions like “Meet this sweet rescue – inspired by Ada! #AdaWolfPack”

Promotion & Content Creation Guidelines

To honor Ada and keep the project professional—especially with eyes like Toly's on us—focus on authentic, heartfelt content. Our narrative—celebrating a real shelter dog and supporting animal welfare—is powerful on its own. Promote $ADA by emphasizing community, charity, and fun without shortcuts.

  • Heartfelt & Original Content: Create posts, graphics, and promotions that come straight from the heart. Share personal stories about shelter dogs, why Ada inspires you, or how the community can help real animals. Avoid anything that feels lazy or generic—our story is too good for that.
  • No AI-Generated Material: Do not use AI for images, lyrics, posts, or any content related to Ada. We're investing in real artists to keep things clean, professional, and true to the spirit. AI often doesn't capture Ada's essence and can make us look unprofessional. Stick to hand-crafted or real photos/art.
  • Professional Presentation: Everything we post or share should look legit and high-quality. Use only the official logos (top.png, adalogo.png, bottom.png) and assets from adaonsol.com. Avoid generic crypto/stock imagery that doesn't represent Ada authentically.
  • Acceptable Topics to Promote: Highlight Ada's story, shelter dog adoptions, donations to animal welfare, fun memes tied to real Solana vibes, #AdaWolfPack community actions, and Toly's original tweets. Encourage bull posts that are sincere—e.g., "Why $ADA reminds me of my own rescue dog and how we're making a difference."
  • How to Do It Better: Brainstorm from personal passion: Write about walking shelter dogs, sharing adoption success stories, or creative ways to support vets/medicine for animals. Collaborate with artists for custom graphics. If you're on the team (like web/graphics roles), lead with examples that inspire others. Always DYOR and remind folks it's a memecoin for fun, not financial advice.
  • Team Expectations: As team members listed on the site, set the standard—post stuff that's genuine and elevates the project. If unsure, check with the project lead before sharing in TG or X.
$ADA is a community memecoin with no intrinsic value or expectation of financial return. There is no formal roadmap or utility. Built for fun, memes, and supporting shelter dogs. Always DYOR. Not affiliated with Solana Labs or Anatoly Yakovenko.
